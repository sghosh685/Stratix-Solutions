GUESS WHAT?

How To Play

The goal of the game is to guess the hidden word before running out of attempts. Each game consists of a randomly chosen word from a chosen theme, and players have five attempts to guess the word. If a player guesses a letter that is not in the word, they lose an attempt. If they use a hint, they also lose an attempt. However, if a player guesses a correct letter, they receive a score and continue guessing. If a player guesses the word correctly, they earn five bonus points. The player with the highest score after a set number of rounds wins the game.

ABOUT

Guess What is a fun and challenging word-guessing game that can be played by anyone. The game is available in multiple themes, such as sports, computers, cities, and food, and allows players to test their knowledge in various categories.

This game was created by a team of developers with a passion for creating fun and engaging games. Our goal was to create a game that is both challenging and educational, and we believe that we have achieved that with Guess What.
