Rules

The goal of the game is to guess the hidden word before running out of attempts. Each game consists of a randomly chosen word from a chosen theme, and players have five attempts to guess the word.

If a player guesses a letter that is not in the word, they lose an attempt. If they use a hint, they also lose an attempt. However, if a player guesses a correct letter, they receive a score and continue guessing.

If a player guesses the word correctly, they earn five bonus points. The player with the highest score after a set number of rounds wins the game.
